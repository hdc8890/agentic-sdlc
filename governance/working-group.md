# AI Platform Working Group

## Purpose

Maintain the shared Agentic SDLC platform model and resolve integration issues across implementations.

## Responsibilities

- steward the semantic contracts
- review proposed changes to interfaces and schemas
- approve breaking changes and version strategy
- coordinate alignment between orchestration, memory, evaluation, and experience owners

## Suggested participants

- orchestration owners
- capability owners
- experience owner or delegate
- platform architect or repo maintainer

## Decision scope

The working group should review:

- contract changes under `docs/specs/` and `schemas/`
- conformance requirements
- new adapter patterns that may affect shared abstractions

## Operating principle

Prefer convergence on shared interfaces over standardizing on a single implementation.
